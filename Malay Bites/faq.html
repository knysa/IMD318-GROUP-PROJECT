Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open from Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Saturdays, we are open from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

How can I order from Malay Bites?

You can place an order directly through our website or by calling our hotline. We also have a delivery service through food delivery apps.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 support via email and chat to assist with any issues.
